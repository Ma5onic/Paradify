How to use

  • Find a video clip on Youtube, Vimeo, Daily Motion or on other supported web sites
  • Click the extension. It will redirect you to Spotify Discover. Login with your spotify account to see your playlists (It does NOT keep any user credential)
  • Choose the song you want to add and then choose a playlist.
  • It will add the song to your Spotify playlist
